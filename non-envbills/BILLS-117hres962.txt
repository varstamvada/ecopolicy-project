                    IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES

                               RESOLUTION


 
                   Supporting the people of America.

    Resolved, That the House of Representatives--
            (1) demands an immediate border wall be constructed on the 
        southern border;
            (2) supports, unequivocally, America's sovereignty and 
        territorial integrity;
            (3) urges United States allies and partners to deliver 
        additional and immediate defensive security assistance to help 
        the United States address illegal border crossings;
            (4) commits to continuing to support U.S. Customs and 
        Border Protection and U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement;
            (5) reaffirms its strong support for the security of the 
        United States;
            (6) pledges to bolster United States energy security and 
        reduce its dependence on Russian energy imports;
            (7) underscores the importance of maintaining United States 
        energy independence for the benefit of the American people;
            (8) supports the right of the American people to protest, 
        and demands the immediate release of all those who have been 
        unjustly detained; and
            (9) stands steadfastly, staunchly, proudly, and fervently 
        behind the American people in their fight for the preservation 
        of the Republic.
