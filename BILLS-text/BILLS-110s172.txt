                                 A BILL


 
   To prohibit Federal funding for the Organisation for Economic Co-
                       operation and Development.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Exit the OECD Act of 2007''.

SEC. 2. PROHIBITION OF FUNDING FOR THE ORGANISATION FOR ECONOMIC CO-
              OPERATION AND DEVELOPMENT.

    Notwithstanding any other provision of law, no Federal funds may be 
expended to fund activities or projects undertaken by the Organisation 
for Economic Co-operation and Development.
