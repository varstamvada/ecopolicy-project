                                 A BILL


 
 To provide for a limitation on availability of funds for Joint Items, 
                 Botanic Gardens for fiscal years 2024.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. LIMITATION ON AVAILABILITY OF FUNDS FOR JOINT ITEMS, BOTANIC 
              GARDENS FOR FISCAL YEARS 2024.

    Notwithstanding any other provision of the law, amounts authorized 
to be appropriated or otherwise available for Joint Items, Botanic 
Gardens for fiscal year 2024 may not exceed $14,759,000.
