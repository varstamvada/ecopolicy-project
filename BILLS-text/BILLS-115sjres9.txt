                            JOINT RESOLUTION


 
  Providing for congressional disapproval under chapter 8 of title 5, 
    United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Securities and 
Exchange Commission relating to the disclosure of payments by resource 
                          extraction issuers.

    Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United 
States of America in Congress assembled, That Congress disapproves the 
rule submitted by the Securities and Exchange Commission relating to 
the disclosure of payments by resource extraction issuers (81 Fed. Reg. 
49360 (July 27, 2016)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
