                                 A BILL


 
    To amend the Immigration and Nationality Act to provide for the 
 expedited removal of aliens who are unlawfully present in the United 
States and are residents of Palestinian-administered territories within 
                       Judea and Samaria or Gaza.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Evicting Violent Islamic Criminals 
That Entered Deviously Act'' or the ``EVICTED Act''.

SEC. 2. EXPEDITED REMOVAL FOR UNLAWFULLY PRESENT ALIENS WHO ARE 
              RESIDENTS OF PALESTINIAN-ADMINISTERED TERRITORIES WITHIN 
              JUDEA AND SAMARIA OR GAZA.

    Section 235(b)(1)(A)(iii) of the Immigration and Nationality Act (8 
U.S.C. 1225(b)(1)(A)(iii)) is amended--
            (1) in subclause (I), by inserting ``or subclause (III)'' 
        after ``subclause (II)''; and
            (2) by adding at the end the following:
                                    ``(III) Additional aliens 
                                described.--An alien described in this 
                                clause is an alien who is unlawfully 
                                present in the United States and is a 
                                resident of a Palestinian-administered 
                                territory within Judea and Samaria or 
                                Gaza.''.
