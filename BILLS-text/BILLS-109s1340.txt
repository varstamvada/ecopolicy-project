Public Law 109-75
109th Congress

                                 An Act


 
 To amend the Pittman-Robertson Wildlife Restoration Act to extend the 
 date after which surplus funds in the wildlife restoration fund become 
   available for apportionment. &lt;&lt;NOTE: Sept. 29, 2005 -  [S. 1340]&gt;&gt; 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. AVAILABILITY OF SURPLUS FUNDS IN WILDLIFE RESTORATION FUND.

    Section 3(b)(2)(C) of the Pittman-Robertson Wildlife Restoration Act 
(16 U.S.C. 669b(b)(2)(C) is amended by striking ``2006'' and inserting 
``2016''.

    Approved September 29, 2005.
