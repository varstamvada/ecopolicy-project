                                 A BILL


 
 To designate the Department of Veterans Affairs Outpatient Clinic in 
   Tulsa, Oklahoma, as the ``Ernest Childers Department of Veterans 
                      Affairs Outpatient Clinic''.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. ERNEST CHILDERS DEPARTMENT OF VETERANS AFFAIRS OUTPATIENT 
              CLINIC.

    (a) Designation.--The Department of Veterans Affairs Outpatient 
Clinic in Tulsa, Oklahoma, shall be known and designated as the 
``Ernest Childers Department of Veterans Affairs Outpatient Clinic''.
    (b) References.--Any reference in a law, regulation, map, document, 
record, or other paper of the United States to the outpatient clinic 
referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the 
Ernest Childers Department of Veterans Affairs Outpatient Clinic.
