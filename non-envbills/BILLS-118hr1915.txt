                                 A BILL


 
  To provide for a limitation on availability of funds for Library of 
Congress, Books for the Blind and Physically Handicapped, Salaries and 
                     Expenses for fiscal year 2024.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. LIMITATION ON AVAILABILITY OF FUNDS FOR LIBRARY OF CONGRESS, 
              BOOKS FOR THE BLIND AND PHYSICALLY HANDICAPPED, SALARIES 
              AND EXPENSES FOR FISCAL YEAR 2024.

    Notwithstanding any other provision of the law, amounts authorized 
to be appropriated or otherwise available for Library of Congress, 
Books for the Blind and Physically Handicapped, Salaries and Expenses 
for fiscal year 2024 may not exceed $52,783,000.
