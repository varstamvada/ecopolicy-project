                                 A BILL


 
  To apply the Federal medical assistance percentage determined for a 
     State for fiscal year 2005 for fiscal years 2006 through 2014.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. APPLICATION OF FISCAL YEAR 2005 FEDERAL MEDICAL ASSISTANCE 
              PERCENTAGE FOR FISCAL YEARS 2006 THROUGH 2014.

    Notwithstanding section 1905(b) of the Social Security Act (42 
U.S.C. 1396d(b)), the Federal medical assistance percentage determined 
for a State for fiscal year 2005 shall apply to the State for each of 
fiscal years 2006 through 2014 for purposes of titles XIX and XXI of 
the Social Security Act (42 U.S.C. 1396 et seq., 1397aa et seq.).
