                                 A BILL


 
  To repeal the per-State limitation applicable to grants made by the 
 National Endowment for the Arts from funds made available for fiscal 
                               year 2005.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. REPEALER.

    Section 311(d) of title III of division E of the Consolidated 
Appropriations Act, 2005 (Public Law 108-447) is amended--
            (1) by striking paragraph (2), and
            (2) by redesignating paragraphs (3) and (4) as paragraphs 
        (2) and (3), respectively.

SEC. 2. EFFECTIVE DATE.

    This Act and the amendments made by section 1 shall take effect on 
October 1, 2004.
