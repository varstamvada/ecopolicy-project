                                 A BILL


 
 To reauthorize the Junior Duck Stamp Conservation and Design Program 
                              Act of 1994.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Junior Duck Stamp Reauthorization 
Act of 2005''.

SEC. 2. REAUTHORIZATION.

    The Junior Duck Stamp Conservation and Design Program Act of 1994 
(16 U.S.C. 719 et seq.) is amended--
            (1) by redesignating the first section 6 (16 U.S.C. 719c), 
        relating to authorization of appropriations, as section 7 and 
        moving the section so as to appear at the end of the Act; and
            (2) in section 7 (as redesignated by paragraph (1)) by 
        striking ``2001 through 2005'' and inserting ``2006 through 
        2010''.
