                                 A BILL


 
   To provide for a limitation on availability of funds for Related 
 Agencies, Commission on Fine Arts, National Capital Arts and Cultural 
                     Affairs for fiscal year 2024.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. LIMITATION ON AVAILABILITY OF FUNDS FOR RELATED AGENCIES, 
              COMMISSION ON FINE ARTS, NATIONAL CAPITAL ARTS AND 
              CULTURAL AFFAIRS FOR FISCAL YEAR 2024.

    Notwithstanding any other provision of the law, amounts authorized 
to be appropriated or otherwise available for Related Agencies, 
Commission on Fine Arts, National Capital Arts and Cultural Affairs for 
fiscal year 2024 may not exceed $2,750,000.
