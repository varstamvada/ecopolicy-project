Public Law 109-306
109th Congress

                                 An Act


 
    To amend the John F. Kennedy Center Act to authorize additional 
 appropriations for the John F. Kennedy Center for the Performing Arts 
      for fiscal year 2007. &lt;&lt;NOTE: Oct. 6, 2006 -  [H.R. 5187]&gt;&gt; 

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. JOHN F. KENNEDY CENTER FOR THE PERFORMING ARTS.

    (a) Maintenance, Repair, and Security.--Section 13(a) of the John F. 
Kennedy Center Act (20 U.S.C. 76r(a)) is amended--
            (1) in paragraph (1) by striking ``and'' at the end;
            (2) in paragraph (2) by striking ``, 2006, and 2007.'' and 
        inserting ``and 2006; and''; and
            (3) by adding at the end the following:
            ``(3) $19,100,000 for fiscal year 2007.''.

    (b) Capital Projects.--Section 13(b) of such Act (20 U.S.C. 76r(b)) 
is amended--
            (1) in paragraph (1) by striking ``and'' at the end;
            (2) in paragraph (2) by striking ``, 2006, and 2007.'' and 
        inserting ``and 2006; and''; and
            (3) by adding at the end the following:
            ``(3) $20,000,000 for fiscal year 2007.''.

    Approved October 6, 2006.
