                                 A BILL


 
   To provide for a limitation on availability of funds for Related 
   Agencies, John F. Kennedy Center for the Performing Arts, Capital 
              Repair and Restoration for fiscal year 2024.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. LIMITATION ON AVAILABILITY OF FUNDS FOR RELATED AGENCIES, 
              JOHN F. KENNEDY CENTER FOR THE PERFORMING ARTS, CAPITAL 
              REPAIR AND RESTORATION FOR FISCAL YEAR 2024.

    Notwithstanding any other provision of the law, amounts authorized 
to be appropriated or otherwise available for Related Agencies, John F. 
Kennedy Center for the Performing Arts, Capital Repair and Restoration 
for fiscal year 2024 may not exceed $13,440,000.
