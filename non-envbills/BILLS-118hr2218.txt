                                 A BILL


 
   To provide for a limitation on availability of funds for Related 
 Agencies, Smithsonian Institution: Facilities Capital for fiscal year 
                                 2024.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. LIMITATION ON AVAILABILITY OF FUNDS FOR RELATED AGENCIES, 
              SMITHSONIAN INSTITUTION: FACILITIES CAPITAL FOR FISCAL 
              YEAR 2024.

    Notwithstanding any other provision of the law, amounts authorized 
to be appropriated or otherwise available for Related Agencies, 
Smithsonian Institution: Facilities Capital for fiscal year 2024 may 
not exceed $210,000,000.
