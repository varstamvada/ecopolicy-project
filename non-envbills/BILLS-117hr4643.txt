                                 A BILL


 
To prohibit the admission of aliens to the United States for 10 years, 
                        and for other purposes.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. 10-YEAR BAN ON ADMISSION OF ALIENS TO THE UNITED STATES.

    Beginning on the date of enactment of this Act, and ending on the 
date that is 10 years after such date of enactment, no alien may be 
admitted to the United States.
